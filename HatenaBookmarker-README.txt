HatenaBookmarker

= Overview =

HatenaBookmaker allows you to create a hatena bookmark to your entry
when publishing entries.

= Installation =

 * Download and extract HatenaBookmarker-<version>.zip file.

 * Upload or copy the contents of "plugins" directory into your
   "plugins" directory.

 * After proper installation, you will find "Hatena Bookmarker" plugin
   listed on the "System Plugin Settings" screen.
